Physics

Physics explores the rules that govern how the universe works, from the smallest particles to the vastness of space. It challenges students to question reality, test ideas and understand the forces shaping everyday life. More than equations and experiments, physics builds curiosity, problem-solving skills and a deeper appreciation for how the world fits together.
