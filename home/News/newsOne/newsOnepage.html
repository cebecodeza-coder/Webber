ON CAMPUS

New goals, new semester. Lets Go!

By Ray Ray
Webber and Company

1 DAY AGO

This is the reset button students wait for: a chance to set intentions that feel realistic, not rushed, and goals that reflect who we are now, not who we thought we had to be. With a fresh timetable, new challenges and lessons from last semester in mind, this is about moving forward with focus, momentum and the belief that progress is still possible.


A new semester isn't just a continuation of the last one; it's a checkpoint, a moment to pause and decide how we want to move forward. It's setting goals that go beyond grades—prioritizing balance, protecting our time, and showing up consistently even when motivation fades. With lessons learned from missed deadlines, late nights and small victories, this semester becomes less about perfection and more about intention, growth and following through.
