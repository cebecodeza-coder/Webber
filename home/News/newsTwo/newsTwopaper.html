ON CAMPUS

First Year Orientation 2026

By Ray Ray
Webber and Company

1 DAY AGO

First Year Orientation 2026 is more than a welcome—it's the moment campus starts to feel like home. From navigating lecture halls and learning academic expectations to meeting new people and discovering student life beyond the classroom, orientation sets the tone for the year ahead


It's a mix of information, connection and first impressions, reminding first-years that while the transition may feel overwhelming, they're not doing it alone. Between icebreakers, campus tours and late conversations that stretch beyond the schedule, orientation becomes the foundation of community. It's where confidence begins to replace uncertainty, and where the first memories of university life are made.
