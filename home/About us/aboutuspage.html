About Us

Webber captures the everyday student experience—balancing classes, campus life, and deadlines. We cover the stories that matter, from academic support and events to national news, sharing honest, relatable voices written by students, for students.


Contact Us

Webber is built on conversation and community, and we want to hear from you. Share a story idea, event, perspective, or question—our team is always open to connecting. Webber is strongest when it reflects the voices of the campus it serves.
